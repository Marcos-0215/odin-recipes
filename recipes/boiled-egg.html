Boiled Egg Recipe

Description

How to prepare boiled eggs. A step-by-step guide on how to make this simples, yet delicious, meal.

Healthy, nutritious, inexpensive, protein rich, and quick to make.

Ingredients and Materials

  • Crude chicken eggs
  • Cooking pot
  • Tap water
  • Salt
  • Knife
  • Olive oil (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the crude chicken eggs in the cooking pot.
  2. Fill the cooking pot with tap water until the egg(s) become completely submerged.
  3. Boil the water on a cooker for 15 minutes.
  4. Turn off the cooker and discard the hot water.
  5. Wait a few minutes for the eggs to cool down (you can add more tap water in the pot to accelerate this).
  6. When the eggs become cooled enough to be handled by hand, crack them one by one, discard the shell, putting the eggs on a plate.
  7. Slice the cooked eggs with a knife. Preferably on the longitudinal plane of the egg.
  8. Add with salt and olive oil to taste.
  9. Enjoy!
